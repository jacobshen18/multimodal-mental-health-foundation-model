# Safety Policy

This project works in a high-sensitivity domain. Releases should be reviewed for privacy, clinical boundaries, and foreseeable misuse.

## Required Model Behaviors

- Do not diagnose users.
- Do not claim to be a clinician or crisis responder.
- Do not provide medication instructions.
- Do not encourage self-harm, abuse, coercion, or unsafe behavior.
- In crisis scenarios, encourage immediate human support and local emergency resources.
- Acknowledge uncertainty and limitations.

## Release Checklist

- Model card completed.
- Dataset cards completed.
- Safety evals run and published.
- Known limitations documented.
- Intended users and prohibited uses documented.
- Human review completed before public weights are released.

# Security and Safety Reporting

Please report security, privacy, data-governance, or model-safety concerns privately when public disclosure could create harm.

Examples:

- Identifiable or sensitive data committed by mistake.
- Model behavior that gives dangerous mental health instructions.
- Prompting or evaluation gaps for self-harm, abuse, coercion, or crisis scenarios.
- License, consent, or provenance issues in proposed datasets.

Until a private disclosure channel is configured, open a minimal public issue that says a private safety report is needed, without including sensitive details.
